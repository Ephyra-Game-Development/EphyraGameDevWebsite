Information About Me

My love for games started at a young age and has only grown stronger over the years. Immersing myself in various gaming worlds, I developed a deep appreciation for the art and science behind game development. This passion led me to pursue a degree in Computer Games Programming, where I graduated with First Class Honours from De Montfort University.

Throughout my academic journey and beyond, I've honed my skills in programming languages such as C, C++, Visual Basic, Python, and Java. My extensive experience with game development tools like Unity has allowed me to bring creative concepts to life. I've led numerous projects, from creating fully functional 2D games to developing innovative game mechanics, always striving for excellence and engaging gameplay.

My background includes a balance of theoretical knowledge and practical application, enabling me to excel in both coursework and real-world projects. This blend of skills and experience has shaped me into a versatile game programmer and creative director, ready to tackle new challenges and create captivating gaming experiences.

As I continue my journey in the world of game development, I am excited to push the boundaries of what's possible and share my love for games with others through my work.
